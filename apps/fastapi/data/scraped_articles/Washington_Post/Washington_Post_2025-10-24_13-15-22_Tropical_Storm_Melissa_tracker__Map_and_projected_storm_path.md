Tropical Storm Melissa has formed in the Caribbean Sea and is on a path toward Jamaica and Haiti. It is expected to bring tropical storm-strength rain and winds to multiple Caribbean Islands by Thursday or Friday and later grow into a hurricane. Hurricane watches cover southern Haiti, including Port-au-Prince, while a tropical storm watch is in effect for Jamaica.
